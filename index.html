welcome to lighthouse

lighthouse cafe ...

Bringing you the finest in Brazilian coffee blends since 2007

As our name implies, Lighthouse Cafe is an early morning beacon for the neighborhood we serve. Providing a bit of comfort ( and caffeine! ) to get you going in the morning, or to keep you going any time of day !

about us

Our beans are sourced from the South-Eastern Brazilian State of Minaes Gerais. The hot and humid climate there produces a sweet, sometimes citrusy coffee that can have hints of caramel or chocolate.

~ ~

source

Lighthouse Cafe uses a specialty bean blend know as Mundo Novo. Typically a dark roast, this unique coffee blend is medium bodied and our specific roast is sweet with a smooth, smoked caramel finish.

~ ~

flavor

Here at Lighthouse, we donate 5% of every cup of coffee sold to a charity or cause of our customer's choice. Every month, we hold a members-only vote to decide where our donations will go !

~ ~
